Entry 1 - Bastien

Bastien

A student in Multimedia and Internet professions who has chosen the option development option of this training. A curious person, invested in what he enjoys and creative. He is overflowing with a good mood, and will not hesitate to share this with the people he meets. He is a good listener, autonomous and has a sense of organization. He can also work in a team.

He loves to draw, edit photos, video and audio in his spare time. He likes to create videos and games to entertain others. He knows how to use some of the of the Adobe suite of software including Photoshop, Illustrator, Audition, Premiere Pro, After Effects, InDesign and more! He also often uses Audacity to edit audio for some of his some of his projects. Not to mention Visual Studio Code where he codes sites like this one using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

He has created this portfolio to present his projects to his readers who will be able to know him better.

The graphic style of this site was greatly inspired by the game "Inscryption".

Dataviz

Entry 6 - Mods

New character

In a certain game, you can play several different predefined characters. What if we change one of them?

So he redid all the "Sprites" of a character in the game, and aligned them correctly, changed lines of code so that his name would be different in the game and the animations would work well. He also changed some sound effects specific to the character.

Said game is "Spelunky 2".

Music change

In another game, there were several pieces of music that he wanted to integrate.

So he learned to code with the language the game uses to add custom music. He modified these tunes to make them loop perfectly and repeat without shocking transitions.

Said game is "Don't Starve Together".

Entry 7 - Level Design

Mini Game

Bastien has created several mini-games, mostly for the game "Team Fortress 2" but not only.

Here is one of the mini-games he created for "Team Fortress 2", this mini-game consists of placing the players on the square squares on the ground. These squares are destroyed randomly. The players who fell down are then moved on platforms of various mini-games that they must complete in order to get back to the main platform.
