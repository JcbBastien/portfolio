Entry 2 - Projects

Chinese Portrait

Here is one of Bastien's projects, a Chinese portrait.

For this project, Bastien had to create a website that had to represent a portrait of himself in the form of a comparison. He had to ask himself the question "if I were a [...]" and answer it as well as to add a reasoning to it.

He also had to add an additional section allowing users to add their own sections to the site.

Chinese Portrait

Dataviz

Here is another one of those projects that was created with fellow student Killian G.

This project consisted of creating a data visualization site from an API, data put into a Json table and to be collected and visualized on the site.

For this project, he used the d3 library to facilitate the production of his code. But he is still not entirely satisfied with his code, he says; "If it were possible to continue working on it, I would transform several lines into a function to reduce the code considerably".

The visual part of the site was thought and realized by his friend Killian.
